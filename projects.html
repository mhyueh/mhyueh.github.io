Surface Parameterization

A parameterization of a surface $\mathcal{M}$ is a bijective mapping $f$ that maps $\mathcal{M}$ onto a simply-shaped planar region $\mathcal{D}$. Surface parameterizations have been widely applied to digital geometry processing tasks, such as surface registration, surface remeshing and surface texturing.
